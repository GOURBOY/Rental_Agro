Tractors

A tractor is a strong work vehicle used for farming. It usually has a seat for only the driver, and can be used to pull many different tools or trailers.

Harvestors

Mechanical Combine harvesters are of two basic types, strippers and pickers. Stripper-type harvesters strip the entire plant of both open and unopened bolls along with many leaves and stems.

Fertilizer Spreader

The spreaders are machines used for the distribution of solid, granular fertilizers and seeds in the field. They can be used whether with tractors of small/medium power.

See Profile

PLOUGH

A Plough is an agricultural implement designed to prepare the soil for planting by turning it over and breaking it up. It typically consists of a set of ploughshares or blades attached to a frame, which is drawn through the soil by a tractor.

See Profile

CULTIVATOR

A Cultivator is an agricultural machine used to cultivate and prepare the soil for planting and weed control. It typically consists of rotating blades or tines that loosen the soil, break up clods, and uproot weeds.

See Profile

SEED DRILL

A Seed drill is an agricultural implement designed for precise and efficient planting of seeds. It consists of a seed hopper, metering mechanisms, and a series of coulters or openers that create furrows in the soil for seed placement.

See Profile

Rotarvators

A Rotary tiller, commonly known as a Rotavator, is a farm implement used for soil cultivation and preparation. It consists of rotating blades or tines that dig into the soil, breaking it into smaller particles and mixing in organic matter.

See Profile

Agriculture Labours

Agricultural labor refers to the workforce involved in various tasks related to farming and agricultural activities. These laborers engage in a wide range of activities, such as planting, harvesting, tilling, weeding, pest control, and livestock management

Agriculture Drone

Agriculture drones, also known as agricultural UAVs (Unmanned Aerial Vehicles), are specialized remote-controlled aircraft used in the agricultural sector. They are equipped with various sensors and cameras to capture data and images.
